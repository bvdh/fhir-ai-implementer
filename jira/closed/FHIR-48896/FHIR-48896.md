# FHIR-48896: Make clear that "subscription support" means the notification bit, not the maintenance part

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Lloyd McKenzie
- **Created**: 11/25/24
- **Description**: In the decision tree when we talk about polling vs. subscriptions, some might read "do you support subscriptions?" as meaning that there's a need to support the full subscription stack - exposing the supported list of subscription topics, being able to dynamically create new subscriptions, as well as the ability to send and receive subscription notifications.  However, all we really care about in making the decision is that last bit.  It's entirely possible that the discovery of potential subscriptions and the creation of the actual subscription could happen out-of-band.  Even in this situation, subscriptions are still typically preferable to polling.

We should update this portion of the decision tree to make the above clear.
