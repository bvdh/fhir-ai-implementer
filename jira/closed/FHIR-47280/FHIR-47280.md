- **Description**: In the subscriptions related pages a number of different concepts are used: Subscriptions, subscription provider, Subscriber, Subscription, support for Subscriptions, Subscription resource, Subscriber, Subscription Framework, the abstract concept of a subscription, an active Subscription and SubscriptionTopic, ...

The specification could benefit from less terms and the use of terms that allow the reader to differentiate more easily between the Resources and the more abstract/active concepts.

This could be done by always use hyperlinks for the resources and add the word resource when possible. Add all terms to section 3.9.1 (Subscription Framework, Subscription Mechanism).

Renaming some of the sections (e.g. Creating a Subscription -&gt; subscribing to notifications).
