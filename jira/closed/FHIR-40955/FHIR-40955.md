- **Description**: The description says:
The targetProfile and binding constraints on the CodeableReference apply to the CodeableReference.reference or the CodeableReference.concept respectively as appropriate and they SHALL NOT be specified directly on the .reference or .concept elements.

When we have experience with implementation practice, we will evaluate changing the SHOULD NOT to a SHALL NOT.
The last sentence is confusing and should be removed. It made sense when the first sentence said SHOULD NOT, but FHIR-39176 resulted in the SHOULD NOT being changed to SHALL NOT. It seems the following sentence should have been removed as part of that same change (but wasn't).
