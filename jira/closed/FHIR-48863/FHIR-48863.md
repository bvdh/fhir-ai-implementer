# FHIR-48863: Do we need a Group container?

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 11/18/24
- **Description**: In FHIR-47496 (and others) we introduced a household as the center around which the data is centered. A Family is represented as a Group. This works very much in the same way as we gather information Patients.

For Patients these are contained in a Patient compartment which gives meaning and scope to resources such as RelatedPerson.

There is no such concept for families. We should discuss and decide whether we need to introduce a Group/family compartment that then could be used to scope operations and restrict access.
