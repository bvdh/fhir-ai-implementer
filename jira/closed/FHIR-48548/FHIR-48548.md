### 2. Ward Weistra - 10/8/24
Security: Public

Note that some R4 IGs currently show in the QA page to have a dependency on the R5 `hl7.fhir.uv.extensions` package: https://hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-hrex/2024Sep/qa.html. The IG Publisher rightfully reports an error on this.

This is what subpackages like `hl7.fhir.uv.extensions.r4` have been created for, and a direct dependency on that makes more sense: https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/fhir-military-service/qa.html

(The careful reader will note that the R4 version of the extensions package currently includes R5 resources, but that's being fixed in next release: https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-46234)
