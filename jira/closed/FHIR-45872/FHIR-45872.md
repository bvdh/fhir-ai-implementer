- **Description**: Lots of implementers are used to the idea of "workflow" statuses being present on the Request resource rather than being managed separately via Task.  And so they migrate to using extensions rather than representing the workflow as they should.  We need a section that clearly explains why Task is necessary (in most cases), why workflow shouldn't be tracked on Requests, as well as some examples of how implementers can/should manage situations where their legacy environment has a single concept that munges the 'authorization' and 'fulfillment' together (contained Task, transaction Bundle, etc.)

Suggest putting a detailed explanation on Request and then linking to it from the various Request resources (as well as Task).
