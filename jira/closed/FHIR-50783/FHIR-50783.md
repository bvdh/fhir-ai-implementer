- **Description**: What is the plan for the codeSystem of RESTful interactions? It is currently marked as experimental. It is also unclear that this has been carefully designed as there are some concepts with Lvl 1 and 2 (search and history); but others are flat at 1 (all others).
- **Issue Type**: Change Request
- **Key**: FHIR-50783
- **Related Page(s)**: REST (http)
- **Related URL**: http://build.fhir.org/codesystem-restful-interaction.html
- **Reporter**: John Moehrke
- **Resolution**: Persuasive with Modification
- **Resolution Description**: 1. It's definitely an error for this to be marked as experimental.  We will remove that flag.

2. The hierarchy is correct as defined.  However, we will mark the 'history' and 'search' codes as 'abstract' as they don't actually reflect real interactions, merely convenient groupings of a collection of interactions to make it easy for implementers to say "I do all of these".  At present, we don't see a strong use-case for other grouping concepts.  Therefore, we'll leave the hierarchy alone.
- **Status**: Applied
- **Summary**: RESTful interactions codesystem is experimental
