# FHIR-44052: Representation of Draft (FMM 0) is unclear and confusing

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 1/22/24
- **Description**: https://hl7.org/fhir/6.0.0-ballot1/versions.html#maturity 
The representation of Draft (FMM 0) is often confusing to those new to FHIR and require them to ask to verify the intent.  FMM 0 is never specifically defined or attributed to Draft - it is only implied.  The FMM Levels table begins with "Draft(0)   the artifact has been published on..." Then FMM 1 is defined, referencing FMM 0 and not Draft: "FMM 1    FMM0 + the artifact produces no warnings...".  There should be a more clear declaration of the FMM 0 &lt;--&gt; Draft relationship.
