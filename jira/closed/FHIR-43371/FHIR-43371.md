# FHIR-43371: Guide to resources is out of date

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 12/13/23
- **Description**: This page doesn't appear to have been updated in several releases, and is missing the majority of resources. It isn't clear how the intent of this page different from many of the other resource/module list pages.

The intent of this page should be clarified, and the page should be updated to reflect that intent, and the full list of current resources.
- **Issue Type**: Change Request
- **Key**: FHIR-43371
- **Related Artifact(s)**: DomainResource
- **Related Page(s)**: Guide to Resources (resourceguide)
- **Related URL**: https://build.fhir.org/resourceguide.html
- **Reporter**: Elliot Silver
- **Resolution**: Persuasive with Modification
- **Resolution Description**: 1. We will change "This page describes the resources and their functional intent in more detail to assist implementers to understand their purpose and scope, and their supporting classifications." to

"This page describes a subset of common clinical terms and identifies the resources most likely to be used to represent such information."

2. We will add Observation as a candidate resource for Chief Complaint and Family History

3. We will remove the sentence "RIM Mappings (todo)" from the end.
- **Status**: Applied
- **Summary**: Guide to resources is out of date
- **Work Group**: FHIR Infrastructure
