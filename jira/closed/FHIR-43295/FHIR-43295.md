# FHIR-43295: Clarify that document and message "must link" requirements are met through RESTful references

- **Description**: This has long been a presumption, but isn't explicitly stated.  Allowing any other type of reference significantly increases complexity for software processing documents and messages (and breaks most existing software).  As well, I can't think of a good reason to allow multiple reference resolution mechanisms in a document (and resolution via business identifier is tricky and often requires business knowledge, making it unrreliable).

## Comments

### 1. Elliot Silver - 11/29/23
Security: Public

I presume the intent is to only require that references to resources in the Bundle have to be RESTful; references outside the Bundle would have no such requirement.

### 2. Lloyd McKenzie - 11/29/23
Security: Public

Right.  The requirement that "all resources in the Bundle must be linked" would need to be met by RESTful references.  Stuff outside the Bundle could be referenced in whatever way you like.  And within the Bundle, if you wanted to include business identifier references too for some reason, that'd be fine.
