- **Description**: Interoperability specifications should not dictate how systems store or display information, rather, they should dictate how systems interoperate data. 

The category of requirements named "Data Expectations" here: https://confluence.hl7.org/pages/viewpage.action?pageId=131040977#SupportHandling-DataExpectation, should not be introduced into the formal/discrete definition of MustSupport. 

 

No doubt there are functional requirements placed upon systems for how they display and store data, including interoperated data. Those functional requirements should not be part of the discrete MustSupport methods.
