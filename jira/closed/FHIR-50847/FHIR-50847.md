- **Resolution Description**: "reserved names" is not clear wording for the desire of the authors. The intention is to clarify that those uses of the underscore character as a prefix can be used as a hint when reading names of interactions and parameters.  E.g., that {{_id}} is intended to apply to *all* resources and not a subset of them.
