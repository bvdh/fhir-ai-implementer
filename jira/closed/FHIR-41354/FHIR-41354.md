- **Description**: Example of VS that references CS that is published as part of FHIR core and that FHIR core publication does not list as a VS that uses the CS: http://terminology.hl7.org/ValueSet-v2-0962.html

I understand that this CS is in FHIR core because a VS that draws codes from it has a required binding to Device.status.  I just don't think that this is a universally acceptable reason to keep CS resources in FHIR core.  There are many codes that may end up in VS with required bindings in FHIR.  That doesn't mean that the corresponding CS should be part of FHIR core in every case.
