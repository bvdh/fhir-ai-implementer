- **Description**: The links for v3, RIM, and workflow mappings ending in hl7.org do not correctly link to the appropriate information. 

For example, on the mappings for CodeSystem (https://build.fhir.org/codesystem-mappings.html), Bundle (https://build.fhir.org/bundle.html), Binary (https://build.fhir.org/binary.html), CapabilityStatement (https://build.fhir.org/capabilitystatement.html) the links fail. 

The displayed URLs lack the .HTML at the end, so the link fails when the user cuts and pastes from the displayed link.
