# FHIR-50352: Allow valueAlternatives on more than primitives

- **Description**: For valueAlternatives, it'd be nice to be able to say "send at least one component, or give me one of these extensions" rather than needing to slice and enumerate the specific extensions that need to be present. Slicing really bulks up the snapshot rendering and is uglier to look at than just having a list of valueAlternatives.

Suggest changing the extension as follows:

Short from "Extensions that are allowed to replace a primitive value"

to "Extensions that are allowed to replace a value/content"

Definition from

"Specifies a list of extensions that can appear in place of a primitive value."

to

"Specifies a list of extensions that can appear in place of a value (for primitive types) or non-extension child elements (for complex types and backbone elements)."

Change the usage note sentence that says "...it SHALL have either a value or at least one of the listed extensions"

to "...it SHALL have at least one of the listed extensions, a value (if primitive), or a non-extension child element (if a complex type or backbone element)"
