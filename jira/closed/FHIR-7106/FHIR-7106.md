# FHIR-7106: 2015May core #165 - Add List.author

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 5/10/15
- **Description**: Existing Wording: n/a

         Proposed Wording: Need to add Author to List resource.

         Comment:

         The FHIR List needs to support the optional concept of Author.  As the text element of List is used to represent the authenticatable narrative content of a Section, the author of the narrative needs to be captured.  When the content is a mix of free text and narrative derived from resources contained in the list, then the author may still be required. When the narrative is derived completely from the list of resources, then the author may be omitted, although the software or transform used to create the narrative may want to be tracked.
