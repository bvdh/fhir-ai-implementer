# FHIR-42959: Identifier.system UUIDs cannot be registered in HL7 OID Registry

- **__EMPTY_1**: Public
- **__EMPTY_2**: Corey Spears to review the latter part of the ticket to see if there are any other considerations that need to be made and put together a possible resolution.

"Also consider adding an explanation of how a NamingSystem resource would be constructed for that UUID, namely, NamingSystem.url should match the Identifier.system if it starts with "urn:uuid:". Also discourage use of urn:uuid across institutional boundaries if there is no registry for them that is well-known to all parties."
- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Comments**: 4/15/24
- **Created**: 10/17/23
- **Description**: Remove "and UUIDs" from the following line because UUIDs cannot be registered in the HL7 OID registry.

" If used, OIDs and UUIDs may be registered in the HL7 OID registry   "

Also consider adding an explanation of how a NamingSystem resource would be constructed for that UUID, namely, NamingSystem.url should match the Identifier.system if it starts with "urn:uuid:". Also discourage use of urn:uuid across institutional boundaries if there is no registry for them that is well-known to all parties.
- **Issue Type**: Change Request
- **Key**: FHIR-42959
- **Related Page(s)**: Datatypes
- **Related Section(s)**: 2.1.28.0.13
- **Related URL**: http://hl7.org/fhir/R5/datatypes.html#identifier
- **Reporter**: Paul Denning
- **Resolution**: Persuasive with Modification
- **Resolution Description**: Will change "If used, OIDs and UUIDs may be registered in the [HL7 OID registry  !https://hl7.org/fhir/R5/external.png!|http://hl7.org/oid] and SHOULD be registered if the content is shared or exchanged across institutional boundaries."

to

"If being exchanged across institutional boundaries, OIDs and UUIDs SHOULD be available to communicating participants in a shared registry.  The [HL7 OID registry  !https://hl7.org/fhir/R5/external.png!|http://hl7.org/oid] may be used for this purpose for OIDs.  [https://terminology.hl7.org|http://tx.fhir.org/] may also be used to register NamingSystems for identifiers of broad international or national interest."
- **Status**: Applied
- **Summary**: Identifier.system UUIDs cannot be registered in HL7 OID Registry
- **Work Group**: FHIR Infrastructure
