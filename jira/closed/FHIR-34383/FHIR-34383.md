# FHIR-34383: Discourage/prohibit mustSupport at profile base element.

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 11/26/21
- **Description**: The use of mustSupport=true on the base element of a profile (including an extension), i.e, on the resource being profiled, or on Extension is unfriendly to users of the profile/extension, and may be unclear.

We should warn about or prohibit this use both in the spec and in the tooling, i.e. IG publisher.
