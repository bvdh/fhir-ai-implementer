- **Description**: The link provided when clicking the chain next to the Quantity subsection on the Datatypes examples page is https://hl7.org/fhir/datatypes-examples.html#count, while other links to that section on the page (e.g. in the diagrams at the top of the page) use https://hl7.org/fhir/datatypes-examples.html#Quantity.

All links and anchors on the page Datatypes pages should be reviewed and corrected if needed.
