# FHIR-40628: eld-11 constraint for ElementDefinition.binding precludes CodeableReference

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 3/17/23
- **Description**: The current test seems to imply that CodeableReference datatypes cannot have a binding, but surely a binding makes sense for the .concept portion of this DataType?

 

binding.empty() or type.code.empty() or type.code.contains(":") or type.select((code = 'code') or (code = 'Coding') or (code='CodeableConcept') or (code = 'Quantity') or (code = 'string') or (code = 'uri') or (code = 'Duration')).exists()
