- **Description**: Consider the following improvements for the CanonicalResource:

for CanonicalResource.copyright 

change short definition to “intellectual property ownership, may include restrictions on use”
change definition to “A copyright statement relating to the Evidence and/or its contents. Copyright statements are legal notices of intellectual property ownership and may include restrictions on the use and publishing of the Evidence.”

for CanonicalResource.copyrightLabel 

correct the typo of resctricted. to restricted

In addition, suggest that these changes be rolled through all canonical resources in FHIR, given that this is the pattern for these elements.
