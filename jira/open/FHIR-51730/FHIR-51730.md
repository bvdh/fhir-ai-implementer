# FHIR-51730: Remove chaining support in CompartmentDefinition

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 8/12/25
- **Description**: None of the defined compartments use search chaining.

CompartmentDefinitions are only permitted to be defined by HL7. Therefore, our defined compartments are the complete (current set) of compartmentdefinitions, and represent all usage of the resource. Since search chaining is not exercised in our definitions, they are not used anywhere.

Remove the unused capability. 

Alternatively, document and provide examples of its use.
