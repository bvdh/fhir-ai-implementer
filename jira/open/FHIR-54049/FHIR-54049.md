# FHIR-54049: SubscriptionTopic cannot indicate required filters or required payload

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 1/13/26
- **Description**: I'm not aware of a way for a server to indicate required filters or content shapes (presumably via SubscriptionTopic).

This is helpful for implementers to communicate their level of support. But this is also important for IGs so that they can define more specific/realistic SubscriptionTopic expectations for implementers.

In particular

	I'd expect many SubscriptionTopics to require a filter on a single specific patient (or some similar requirement that limits context)
	I'd expect many SubscriptionTopics to require a Subscription.content of id-only or {{empty }}for security reasons



Both of these could be addressed by adding fields to SubscriptionTopic, e.g.:

	(boolean 0..1) SubscriptionTopic.trigger.canFilterBy.required
	(code 0..*): SubscriptionTopic.supportedPayload
