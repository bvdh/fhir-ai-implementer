# FHIR-53789: Clarify list Scope & Usage language

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 1/5/26
- **Description**: I see that the sentence “A list cannot be the subject of an intervention or a direct action” was added to the Scope and Usage section. Could you clarify what is meant by “subject of an intervention” in this context, particularly given the use of List.mode = changes to communicate reconciliation outcomes (e.g., discharge medication lists with added and deleted items)? If the intent is simply that Lists are not themselves clinical targets, but rather artifacts that communicate or support workflow actions, it would be helpful to state that more explicitly, especially since this sentence appears in the opening paragraph.
