In `TestScript.metadata.capability`: both `.required` and `.validated` have cardinality 1..1, while both have invariant tst-4 applied to them:



tst-4
Rule
TestScript metadata capability SHALL contain required or validated or both.
capability.required.exists() or capability.validated.exists()





Metadata capability cannot contain either of the two elements, it always has to contain both. Don't know what the intended use is and whether the cardinalities or the invariant are wrong.
