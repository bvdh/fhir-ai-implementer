# FHIR-56003: Extension request for Attachment Content Encoding

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 3/3/26
- **Description**: It is common to represent data in standard encodings other than 'raw', specifically for compression.

`Attachment` has a `contentType`, but no equivalent of `contentEncoding` when including `data`.

Request the addition of a `content-encoding` extension on either `Attachment` or `base64Binary` to allow representation of encoding types.

Suggest an extensible coding, with at least: `deflate`, `gzip`, `zlib` as a starter set.
