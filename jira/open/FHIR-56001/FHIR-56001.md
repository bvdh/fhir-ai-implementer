# FHIR-56001: Expand Rendered Value to Ratio

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 3/3/26
- **Description**: Ratios may have specific renderings that are preferred and cannot be tagged individually on the numerator and denominator elements safely.

E.g., a numerator of 33 and denominator of 99 can together be displayed as `1/3`, but just replacing 33 with 1 can be problematic.

Request adding `Ratio` to the context of the rendered-value extension.
