First draft mapping to IEEE 11073-10207 (SDC BICEPS):





Extension: DeviceAlertDetection
(Limit)AlertConditionDescriptor, (Limit)AlertConditionState


alertCode
(Limit)AlertConditionDescriptor/Type


priority
If present, (Limit)AlertConditionState/ActualPriority;
otherwise (Limit)AlertConditionDescriptor/Priority


effective
-


activationState
(Limit)AlertConditionState/ActivationState


limitRange

### 4. Javier Espina - 2/11/26
Security: Public

For discussion on the mapping to IEEE 11073-10201 (Classic DIM):

AFAIK, it is not really mappable because -10201 does not model the concept of (in)activation states independently of an actual alert condition having existed.

However, FWIW, here's an idea of how the mapping may look like:



Extension: DeviceAlertDetection
AlertCondition, AlertState


alertCode
AlertCondition/alert-code


priority
AlertCondition/alert-type


effective
-


activationState
-


limitRange    
Limit-Specification
