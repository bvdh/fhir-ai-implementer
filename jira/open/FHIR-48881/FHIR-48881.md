# FHIR-48881: Artifacts menu doesn't work once you are inside the extensions registry

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 11/21/24
- **Description**: When you go from the main FHIR page via the Artifacts menu to the Extensions Pack, the Artifacts menu no longer works.

I realise that the child packs (IGs) do not know about the parent menu, but something could be added such as "go back" or "home", to give a clue why the menu is not available. Or it could be removed, greyed out etc.

It is easy to use the back button, but if you try the menu without realising that you are really in another IG, it is confusing to see that the menu that you just used to get here doesn't work any more.
