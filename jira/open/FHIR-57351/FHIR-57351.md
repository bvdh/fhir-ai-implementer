# FHIR-57351: Definition of 'denied' is incorrect

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 5/19/26
- **Description**: The definition of 'denied' includes the sentence: "This may also be known as 'rejected'."

This is incorrect. 'rejected' is NOT an adjudication outcome, it means that a claim failed a clean claim edit and was not adjudicated at all. We need to remove that sentence.
