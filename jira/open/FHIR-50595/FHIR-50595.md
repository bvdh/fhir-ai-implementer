# FHIR-50595: Does the Maturity level of TestScript accurately reflect real-world usage and review?

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 5/1/25
- **Description**: We may not have all the information, but this comment may still be useful to start a conversation. 

Our internal reviewers were surprised to see a Maturity level of 4 applied to this specification since we have not heard of any examples of organizations we work with having tried out this technology, and some details from within the spec feel underdeveloped.

Furthermore, the related specification pages, such as "Testing FHIR", "TestReport", and "TestPlan" are all marked as having Maturity level 1 or 2. Is Maturity Level 4 an accurate indication of the current usage level of this spec?
