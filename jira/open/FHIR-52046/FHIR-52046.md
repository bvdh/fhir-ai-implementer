# FHIR-52046: TestScript sourceId type.code invariants

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Richard Ettema
- **Created**: 8/28/25
- **Description**: Regarding `tst-7`, `tst-8` and `tst-9`

The invariant says `.sourceId` needs to be present, but not when `.operation.type` is 'transaction'. Isn't this an error? I cannot imagine a case where I would want to transact something, but do not define what.

The other way around, why would 'batch' be absent from this list is this isn't an error? Or am I missing some edge case?

Looking further while 'history' and 'search' are mentioned in the invariants, all level 2 codes in https://hl7.org/fhir/R5/valueset-testscript-operation-codes.html are not. Shouldn't these be included for completeness?
- **Issue Type**: Change Request
- **Key**: FHIR-52046
- **Related Artifact(s)**: TestScript
- **Related Section(s)**: Constraints
- **Related URL**: https://hl7.org/fhir/R5/testscript.html#invs
- **Reporter**: JornDuwel
- **Resolution**: Persuasive
- **Resolution Description**: For tst-7, will change description to: "Operation must have a source id unless the method is one that doesn't always require a payload or the operation type is one that doesn't always require a payload" with FHIRPath of:
sourceId.exists() or method in ('get' | 'delete' | 'head')  or not(type.code in ('create' | 'create-conditional' | 'update' | 'update-conditional' | 'patch' | 'patch-conditional' | 'transaction' | 'batch'))

We will remove the other two because they're already inherited from where Operation is defined.
