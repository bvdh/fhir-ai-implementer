FHIR documents are used for documents that are authored and assembled in FHIR, while the DocumentReference resource is for general references to documents (which may include FHIR documents as well as PDFs, CDAs, etc.)."

to "Note that FHIR defines several structures in this space:
 - this document format (a document bundle containing a Composition resource and potentially other resources) which is for documents authored and assembled in FHIR and where the computable 'content' of the document is found in individual discrete resources.
 - a [DocumentReference resource|https://build.fhir.org/documentreference.html] which is used is for general references to documents (which may include FHIR documents as well as PDFs, CDAs, etc.).

 - a [DiagnosticReport resource]() which is used strictly to compile a collection of diagnostic information and focuses on the discrete data in the report, but may contain or reference a separate rendered version of the report."
