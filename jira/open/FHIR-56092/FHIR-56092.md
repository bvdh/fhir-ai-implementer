# FHIR-56092: Group definitions are not clear (or disjoint)

## Metadata

- **Assignee**: Unassigned
- **Created**: 3/16/26
- **Description**: definitional and conceptual are overlapping.  Enumerated implies all members must be listed.

Propose:



Definitional
The Group.characteristics specified are both necessary and sufficient to determine membership.  Members are represented as FHIR resources. All entities that meet the criteria are considered to be members of the group, whether referenced by the group or not. If members are present, they are individuals that happen to be known as meeting the Group.characteristics. The list cannot be presumed to be complete.








Conceptual
This is identical to Definitional, except that members are entities that cannot be represented as FHIR resources./








Enumerated
The Group.characteristics are necessary but not sufficient to determine membership. Membership can only be confirmed by checking whether the entitity is listed as as a Group.member, though it's possible not all members are listed.
