# FHIR-45021: Datatype Range, definition not clear for age ranges

- **Description**: The current definition of the datatype Range states the following sentence:

"The low and the high values are inclusive and are assumed to have arbitrarily high precision;"

Unfortunately there is a broad variety what "inclusive" for low and high could mean, especially when time-spans are affected like for Observation.referenceRange.age.

We've tried ** to collect examples for the various interpretations in the attached document.

What would be needed is a more precise definition of "inclusive" especially for time spans, ideally with some example to explain it.
