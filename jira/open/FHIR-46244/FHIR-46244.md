- **Description**: When applying the R5 convention for pre-adopting an R5 element (example: http://hl7.org/fhir/5.0/StructureDefinition/extension-Appointment.virtualService) tooling like IGP will elicit errors indicating invalid constrained type (because Appointment.virtualService has a dataType of VirtualServiceDetail only defined in R5). 

 

We are requesting an improvement to the tooling or alternative for modelling that would allow our implementors of the Canadian eReferral/eConsult specification to better prepare to convey the information in a way that will translate well when we move to R6 in the future.
